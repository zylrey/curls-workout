Bicep Curls Workout Plan

Preparation and Guidelines

Warm-Up (5 minutes): Perform arm circles, push-ups (modified if needed), and light cardio like jumping jacks.

Workout Tips:

  • Perform 3 sets of each exercise, resting for 30–60 seconds between sets.
  • Complete the workout 3–4 days per week, allowing at least 1 rest day between sessions.
  • Stretch your chest and shoulders after each session to improve flexibility and recovery.
